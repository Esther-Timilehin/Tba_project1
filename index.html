About TBA

Our mission is to serve as a religious educational institution dedicated to raising envoys of God's presence.

TBA provides a nurturing environment where youth discover their life's purpose and develop their talents through values-based education and mentorship. Emphasizing community engagement and service, TBA equips individuals with life skills, leadership qualities, and a strong support network, preparing them to lead principled lives and make positive contributions to society as ambassadors of God's presence.
